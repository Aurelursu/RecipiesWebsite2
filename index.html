Homemade pancake recipy

This is an easy and reliable recipy when u crave something sweet but you are too lazy to do it.



Necesary ingredients:

  • 2 big eggs or if u dont have big ones u can use 4 smaller ones;
  • 250g of fresh milk;
  • 500ml of fresh milk;
  • 1 spoon of vanilla sugar;
  • a pinch of salt.


These ingredients are easy to find and u may have all of them at your local market.



Preparation

  1. Beat the eggs with the salt until they combine.
  2. Add flour and beat them until there are no more air bublles.
  3. Add the milk little by little until the compozitions becomes a liquid.
  4. Add a liltle bit of the compozition into a hot frying pan and cook it on each side for 1-2 minutes.
